Ibis Project

This was my first ever website, built as a part of the SheCodes Basics course. Here is what we needed to include on our webpage to pass the course: h1, h2, p, div, span, strong, em, br, hr, a, img, button, and from CSS, margin, padding, font-size, font-weight, color, background (with linear-gradient), line-height, box-shadow, border and transition. Design wise the brief was pretty loose but we were given an example site as a guide and the idea was to keep it simple. It is nice to see how much I have learned since building this page.
